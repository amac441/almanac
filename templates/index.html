OVERVIEW:

5years is a free tool to help counselors and job seekers manage and monitor the complex career landscape. It offers a comprehensive toolsuite to help the two groups research, define, and monitor an individual's career plan.

Instead of using Linkedin to list things in your past, use 5years to plot and grow towards your future

FOR CAREER COUNSELORS FOR CAREER SEEKERS FOR STUDENTS
For Counselors

  • Aggregated, up-to-date access to the latest jobs data and learning content
  • Access to Dashboard and Detailed view of all clients
  • Ability to view client's calendars and quickly assign tasks, send messages, and schedule meetings
  • Automated task assignment and followup messaging
  • Macro data on counselees and their progress over time

For Career Seekers

  • Externally managed "to do list" so that you can spend less time analyzing and more time progressing
  • Points and Rewards system for completing key tasks and milestones
  • Access to key individuals to help grow your network
  • Ability to see the "Plans" of your icons, peers, and proteges.
  • Distilled access to the same research tools that professional counselors use to plot your path
  • Dashboards to visualize your progress, learnings, and interests
  • Focus on local, free, and high yeild growth options

For Students

  • Custom access to your College or University's learning curriculum
  • Recurring appointments with Career Counselors
  • Access to University sponsored job boards and research sites

WHY WE'RE HERE

"The achievement of difficult goals entails not only talent but also the sustained and focused application of talent over time." - Mike Matthews

People tend to only focus on the growth of their bank accounts, not the growth of their greatest asset - their skills and knowledge. As a result, only 13% of young adults feel satisfied in their jobs.

The idea is that by reducing the friction between counselors and counselees, and by taking the guessing out of career planning, we can create a more productive, motivated, and satisfied workforce.
